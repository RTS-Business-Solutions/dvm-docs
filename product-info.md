# DVM Product Information

## Dynamic VAT Management (DVM)

### Supplemental Product Overview

## Executive Summary

Dynamic VAT Management (DVM) is an advanced VAT automation module designed for organizations operating across multiple tax jurisdictions.

It enables real-time VAT adjustments based on routes, destinations, and operational context, ensuring compliance without manual intervention.

DVM is built for environments where VAT rules change during operations — such as maritime transport, passenger services, cross-border retail, and international logistics.

The module integrates directly with POS operations and scheduling logic, allowing businesses to maintain accuracy while reducing manual workload.

## Product Overview

Dynamic VAT Management provides a structured framework for managing VAT rules across vessels, routes, legs, and operational timetables.

The system dynamically determines the correct VAT based on journey context and applies tax rules automatically.

The architecture supports both automated and manual control, ensuring operational continuity even in exceptional scenarios.

## Core Capabilities

- Automatic VAT switching based on operational route and timetable
- Timetable-driven VAT logic across jurisdictions
- Manual override through POS commands
- Per-vessel and per-route VAT configuration
- API-ready integration architecture
- Fallback configuration for offline or exception scenarios

## System Architecture

DVM operates through configurable entities including:

- Vessels
- Routes
- Legs
- VAT store groups

Timetables define jurisdiction transitions and allow the POS environment to automatically select correct VAT rules.

This modular architecture allows organizations to scale operations while maintaining consistent tax compliance.

## Timetable Logic

Timetable-based automation enables real-time switching of VAT rules as operations cross borders.

Manual timetables and API-driven timetable imports are supported, allowing organizations to align tax logic with operational scheduling systems.

The system ensures uninterrupted VAT accuracy regardless of operational complexity.

## Integration & Automation

Dynamic VAT Management supports API-based integration with:

- External workflow platforms
- Scheduling systems
- POS ecosystems

This enables advanced orchestration and synchronization of VAT logic with operational data sources.

Integration ensures that VAT management becomes part of a broader automation strategy rather than an isolated function.

## Typical Use Cases

- Passenger ferry and maritime operations
- Cross-border retail environments
- Multi-country logistics and transport
- International POS deployments
- Travel and duty-free operations

## Business Benefits

- Reduced manual VAT handling
- Improved compliance across jurisdictions
- Automated tax accuracy during operations
- Lower operational risk
- Scalable architecture for international growth

## Conclusion

Dynamic VAT Management delivers automated, compliant VAT handling in complex international environments.

It reduces operational overhead, improves accuracy, and ensures that organizations can scale across jurisdictions with confidence.
