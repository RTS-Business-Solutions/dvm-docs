# Privacy Policy

## 1. Introduction

Welcome to RTS Business Solutions.

We are committed to protecting the privacy and security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you engage with our booking page, which is powered by Microsoft Bookings.

## 2. Information We Collect

### Personal Identification Information

This includes:

- Name
- Email address
- Telephone number
- Any other details you provide when booking an appointment

### Booking Details

Information related to your appointment, such as:

- Date
- Time
- Type of service requested

### Communication Data

Any correspondence between us, including:

- Emails
- Messages submitted through our booking page

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

- Manage and fulfil your bookings
- Improve our services and website
- Communicate with you, including sending confirmation and reminder emails
- Comply with legal obligations

## 4. Sharing of Your Information

We do not sell or rent your data.

However, we may share your information with third-party service providers who perform services on our behalf, such as hosting our booking page. All third-party service providers are required to take appropriate security measures to protect your personal information in accordance with our policies.
